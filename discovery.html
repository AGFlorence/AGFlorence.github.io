    A Record of My Discoveries at University

    Week1 - An Introduction to Binary: a Computers +/-

    this week was the first main week of starting my degree course. In this week I attended Lectures on Binary the main method for computers interacting with data. I learnt that Binary is numerical notation system using only 2 number fields rather that numeric which uses 10 number fields

    Week2 - Computer Networking: Protocols IP/TCP

    during this week i started studying network fundamentals starting with the current standards and Protocols of networking. This being IP & TCP, IP is the networking protocol that deals with locating a route through the network to get the packet of information from point A to point B. TCP being a networking Protocol that deals with the delivery method and assuring the data is transferred successfully.

    Week3 - The OSI model: 7 layers of "fun"

    this week was the week i was introduced to the currently used networking model or the OSI model. This being the 7 layer OSI model that is used to explain how the layers of a network build up to complete the whole network. This was quite a boring week of studies but it was useful to learn about atleast.

    Week4 - Boolean Algebra: simplifing by complicating

    during this week my CAR (computer architecture) had begun our work for our 2nd major coursework of the the unit. This was detailed around logical design of computers and its representation. We had to set up a diagram of logic gateways and mathically represent them using algebra

    Week5 - Website Addressing: how to sign-post my site!

    during this week i began work on the navigation elements of my website and as well as this i also attended a lecture on good and bad website addressing and have tryed to follow those points learnt in the lecture to keep the navigation of my website sound.

    Week6 - Usability: Contrast Checking and a terrible colour scheme!

    this was a very interesting week. During this week showcased a draft of this current website to one of my lecturers asking for feedback. Overall it was explained to me that the original color schemes used and the methods of execution of certain functions of my website where not user friendly and imparticular my color scheme did not work very well

    Week7 - Accessibility: Understanding being Blind and Handless!

    during this week in my webF1 practicals i learnt about Accessibility in website design in the practical session we simulated using special goggles what is was like to have tunnel vision and we also tryed to navigate through our website whilst handless as well.

    Week8 - SVG: An In-depth Picture Lecture!

    SVG or scalable vector graphics are a form picture creation in programming that defines the picture format of XML standards. i attended a lecture on this topic. which made it seemed promising and helpful. However having researched slightly more into i decide it was not what i need.

    Week9 - Researched Mobile-Friendly Website design .CSS & .JS!

    during this week i had assessed my website using github.io and found that my website did not scale properly nor was it in anyway Mobile-Friendly to use thus i decided to begin researching in how utilise CSS and .JS to remedy problems i have been having with Mobile-Friendliness and whilst i have found some promising results of this research i am yet to implement anything.
